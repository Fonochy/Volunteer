About us

What is Power Volunteers?


PowerVolunteers.org is a website that collects places around the world that need help

and volunteers can find for places that they want to go , providing opportunities to help

those in need and enhance the quality of life in that places.


Power Volunteers is a service to help link community volunteer needs with volunteers and from

around the world and does not discriminate based on race, religion, gender, ethnicity, or sexual

orientation in posting projects or in encouraging volunteers to serve according to our guidelines.


We've come a long way since we started this journey in 2010.

We hope you'll join the adventure!
